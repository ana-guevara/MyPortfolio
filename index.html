About Me

Hello welcome! I am a Software Engineering undergraduate student at the University of Puerto Rico, Mayagüez Campus. While my curriculum emphasizes back-end development and the technical aspects of software engineering, I’ve developed a strong passion for front-end development and user experience design. Since my program focuses primarily on the back-end, I’ve taken it upon myself to explore and deeply understand the front-end on my own. I strive to combine the technical knowledge from my coursework with my creative, visual mindset to design intuitive, aesthetically pleasing user interfaces. To further tap into my creativity, I often choose electives in the arts, which provide fresh perspectives and enhance my design skills. My ultimate goal is to leverage both my engineering foundation and my artistic passion to contribute something meaningful to society, using technology to positively impact people while continually pushing the boundaries of my creativity.
